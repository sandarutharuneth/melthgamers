The Story behind Melth Gamers

Melth Gamers began as a hobby, and our original channel name was Royal Gamers. When we hit 1000 subscribers, we wanted to alter the name to something more distinctive. As a result, we joined our surnames to form "MELTH" (MELon - THaruneth). After receiving numerous requests to offer daily livestreams on YouTube, we have decided to do so. For the time being, it is our story. We hope you've gained some insight as a result of this.

Meet the team

How Melth Gamers started

Melth gamers began in early 2021, with two of their best buddies. They obtained 1000 subscribers in 6 months by staring at the live feed on YouTube many times a week. Following that, the channel took off like wildfire, particularly in Sri Lanka. Their gaming style and innovative ideas have landed them in this position.
